# MeloFi-SRS

MeloFi is a modern, community-driven music-sharing and discovery platform that connects artists and listeners in one interactive space. It allows users to upload, stream, and share music while offering personalized recommendations, playlists, and social features like likes, comments, and follows. The platform aims to empower independent artists to showcase their talent and help music lovers discover new sounds effortlessly in a clean, engaging, and user-friendly environment.
